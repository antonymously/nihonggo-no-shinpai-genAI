Learn JLPT N4 Grammar: の中で (no naka de)

July 31, 2015

  
Learn Japanese N4 Grammar

Click on image to view full size.

Meaning:
 in; among

Formation:

Noun + の中で

Example sentences:

There are 38 example sentences available for this grammar point.

In his mind, the world has always been divided into two parts.

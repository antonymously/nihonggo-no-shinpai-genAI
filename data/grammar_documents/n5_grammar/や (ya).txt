Learn JLPT N5 Grammar: や (ya)

September 24, 2014

  
Learn Japanese Grammar
, 
Learn Japanese N5 Grammar

Meaning:
 and

や is used to link nouns in an incomplete list.

Formation:

Noun + や + Noun

Example sentences:

There are 32 example sentences available for this grammar point.

Learn JLPT N5 Grammar: たい (tai)

September 21, 2014

  
Learn Japanese Grammar
, 
Learn Japanese N5 Grammar

Meaning:
 want to do something

You use たい to express a desire to do something. The direct object can be marked either by が or を.

Formation:

Verb-ますstem + たい

Example sentences:

There are 65 example sentences available for this grammar point.

私も外に出て歩きたい。
I want to get out and walk too.

人は理解されたい。
People want to be understood.

皆さんに一つ質問をしたい。
Let me ask you a question.

わたしが訴えたいのはこの点だ。
That’s the sort of thing I mean.
watashi ga uttaetai no wa kono ten da.

私は他人から好かれたい。
I want to be loved by others.
watashi wa tanin kara sukaretai.
